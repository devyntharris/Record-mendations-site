Welcome to Record-mendations

—your curated guide to discovering new music through thoughtful, personal album recommendations.

Every review on this site is written by me—an avid music enthusiast and aspiring graphic designer with a deep appreciation for storytelling through sound. My goal is to connect the dots between music, emotion, and creativity, offering a fresh perspective on albums that have impacted me deeply and may resonate with you too.

This site features handpicked album recommendations, categorized into four main genres:

Modern: Contemporary releases spanning various styles, from indie and pop to alternative and beyond.

Oldies: Timeless classics, primarily from the '50s through the '80s, that shaped the music landscape.

Film: Scores and soundtracks that bring cinematic moments to life.

Musicals: Original cast recordings and standout albums from Broadway, West End, and movie adaptations.

Each album review includes:
  • Album Artwork & Spotify Link: An image of the album cover, prominently displayed. When you click on the image, it will take you directly to the album's Spotify page for easy access. This feature ensures that you can instantly immerse yourself in the music with just a click.
  • Top 3 Tracks: My personal favorite tracks from the album, chosen based on their impact on the album’s flow and their emotional or musical resonance. These selections are deeply influenced by what stands out most to me as a listener, and I hope they offer insight into why these songs are memorable.
  • Album Overview: A brief summary of the album’s sound, mood, and overall impact, covering its musical style, emotional tone, and influence. I’ll explore how the album resonates within its genre, its relevance to current trends, and why it stands out as a noteworthy listening experience.
Have an album you love and think I should hear?

I’m always eager to discover new music and hear what others are passionate about. Head over to the Contact page to submit your recommendation, and share the albums that have made an impact on you. I’d love to explore your suggestions and uncover hidden gems that might become new favorites.

Thank you for visiting Record-mendations—I hope you find something new and inspiring to add to your own collection.
